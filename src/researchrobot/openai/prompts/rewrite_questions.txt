We are going to rewrite data questions to be more explict about what
information the user wants to know.

For example:

  Original: How many female household members, ages 0 to 18, living in family
  households who are not related to the head of the household, can be
  identified by analyzing the given data?
  Rewrite: How many people who are female, live in a family household, are between the ages of 0 and 18 and not related to the head of the household?

  Original: What is the median household income in Calhoun County, Georgia for American Indian adults in 2016, adjusted for inflation?
  Rewrite: What is the median income adjusted for inflation for households that are in Calhoun County, Georgia and are headed by an American Indian adults, in 2016?
